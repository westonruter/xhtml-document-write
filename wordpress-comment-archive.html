  10. Leif Halvard Silli
    April 18th, 2010 at 12:16 pm

    If the element one tries to insert contains an attribute with a hyphen, then the element is not inserted at all.

    Usecases: HTML5: data-myname="*", ARIA: aria-describedby="*", HTTP: <meta http-equiv="*" content="*">.

    Example of non-working code:

    <script type="text/javascript">//<![CDATA[
    document.write('<div data-abc=abc >Hello <i>World</i>!</div>');
    //]]></script>

  11. Leif Halvard Silli
    April 18th, 2010 at 2:42 pm

    It is a bug in Resig’s HTMLparser – it doesn’t handle attributes with a hyphen in the name.
